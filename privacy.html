Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and look after your personal information. This is placeholder content for the demo.

1. Who we are

Revtech Automotive is a mechanical workshop based in Christchurch. This policy explains how we handle personal information in line with the Privacy Act 2020.

2. What we collect

When you contact us or book work, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, and details about your vehicle and the work required. We only collect what we need to quote and carry out your job.

3. How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide a quote
  • To book, carry out and invoice your service or repair
  • To contact you about your vehicle or a booking
  • To keep records of work carried out for warranty and servicing history

4. Sharing your information

We don't sell your information. We only share it where needed to complete your job — for example with a parts supplier — or where required by law. Enquiry forms on this site are delivered to us by a third-party form service purely to pass your message on.

5. Storage & security

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure and to protect it from loss, misuse or unauthorised access. We keep records only as long as needed for the purpose collected or as required by law.

6. Your rights

You can ask to see the personal information we hold about you and request a correction if it's wrong. Contact us using the details below and we'll help.

7. Contact

For any privacy question, email revtech.auto@hotmail.co.nz or call 03 964 2765.
